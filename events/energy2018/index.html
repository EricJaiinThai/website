10th Workshop on the CityGML Energy ADE
December 5-7, 2018 · Delft, the Netherlands




The registration for the workshop is now open. Click here to register.



We are organizing the ‘10th International Workshop on the CityGML Energy ADE’ at TU Delft on December 5-7, 2018. The workshop is a collaborative effort of the 3D Geoinformation group, TU Delft, The Netherlands, OGC, and SIG 3D. Within the workshop, we are organizing an open “Knowledge exchange event about energy and the digital built environment” on Thursday, December 6th, 2018 in the afternoon from 14:00 to 18:00 hrs to understand the requirements of potential users and to create collaborations for energy modelling as one potential application of 3D city models.

It can be a great opportunity to gather further requirements, ideas, suggestions, and experiences from users, data producers, software vendors, and scientists involved in energy applications. One of the goals of this event is to bring together experts from different disciplines in order to share experiences on energy-related applications based on digital city models.

Agenda of the Knowledge exchange event:


  • G. Agugiaro (TU Delft): "Welcome and a brief overview of the Energy ADE"
  • J. Benner (KIT): "The Energy ADE in Germany: current situation"
  • P. Cipriano (DedaGroup): "Managing urban energy-related data: Experiences from Italy"
  • T. Hong (L. Berkeley Nat. Lab.): "Modeling coupled urban systems: Data and computing challenges"
  • C.-K. Wang (TU Delft): "Minimizing uncertainty in modelling urban building energy using 3D city models and Bayesian inference"
  • (Pending) Presentation by A. van der Meer (TU Delft, AMS)
  • (Pending) Presentation by Geonovum
  • (Pending) Presentation by City of Rotterdam
