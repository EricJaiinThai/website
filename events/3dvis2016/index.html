3DViz 2016

Workshop on 3D Visualisation
September 27th-28th, 2016 · Delft, the Netherlands






Registration open!!

June 28: The registration for 3DViz 2016 is now open!!

The 3DViz 2016 is being organized by the 3D Geoinformation group, TU Delft, The Netherlands in collaboration with Technical University of Munich. The objective of this workshop is to discuss the storage and visualisation of 3D city models, and aims at sharing associated techniques, methods, uses and points of view. Through this workshop we will explore state of the art web-based standards and technologies and current modern virtual globes like Cesium and 3DCityDB, the current open source solution for storing 3D city models. The workshop aims to provide:

  • Overview of standards and technologies for browser-based 3D visualization
  • Overview of browser-based virtual globe for 3D geo-visualization
  • Fundamental understanding of the software engine CESIUM Virtual Globe
  • 3D city models storage with 3DCityDB
  • Practical experience with CESIUM Virtual Globe & 3DCityDB

For any enquiries about the workshop, please contact Jantien Stoter or Kavisha Kumar.
