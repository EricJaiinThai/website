We had the pleasure of organising [UDMV2015](https://3d.bk.tudelft.nl/events/udmv2015/): the 3rd Eurographics Workshop on Urban Data Modelling and Visualisation.

The event attracted [13 presentations](https://3d.bk.tudelft.nl/events/udmv2015/program.html), and participants from 8 countries. It focused on topics bridging computer graphics and GIS, and it has successfully opened discussions on common challenges and future collaborations. The proceedings of UDMV have just been [published](http://diglib.eg.org/handle/10.2312/13879). The papers have been subject to a double-blind rigorous peer review performed by an [international programme committee](https://3d.bk.tudelft.nl/events/udmv2015/committee.html#program).

We thank everyone for their interest, and we hope to see you next year on UDMV2016!
